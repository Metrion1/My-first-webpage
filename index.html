About Me

During this time, I have been working on transforming myself into an entrepreneur. The main goal is to get my Invention onto the market and be able to build something generational from that. To get to that point I need to fund my Prototype and business. I have tried multiple avenues such as applying for grants and loans, starting a clothing business and becoming an author. I have unfortunately realized that all these avenues require a hefty amount of investment on my end. So, we have come full circle, this is my launch point, to become a software developer, make enough money to fund my dreams and then leave my mark on society no matter how small.
